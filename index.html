List of evergreen Malayalam Movies

1. Manichitrathazhu

List of Cast

  1. Mohanlal
  2. Shobhana
  3. Suresh Gopi
  4. Revathy

Plot

Manichitrathazhu is a 1993 Indian Malayalam-language psychological thriller film directed by Fazil, written by Madhu Muttam, and produced by Swargachitra Appachan. The film stars Mohanlal, Suresh Gopi and Shobana with Nedumudi Venu, Innocent, Vinaya Prasad, K.P.A.C.Lalitha, Sridhar, K.B. Ganesh Kumar, Sudheesh, and Thilakan in supporting roles. The story is inspired by a tragedy that happened in an Ezhava tharavad of Alummoottil meda' a old (Traditional house) located at Muttom (near Haripad), a central Travancore Channar family, in the 19th century. The Story writer Madhu K Panicker or Madhu Muttam himself belong to this Alummoottil Tharavad.

2. Guru

List of Cast

  1. Mohanlal
  2. Shobhana
  3. Suresh Gopi
  4. Vinay Prasad
  5. Nedumudi venu

Plot

In this new world everyone is blind (symbolic of religion). They believe that the vision is a lie and that even talking about it is blasphemy. And so there he saves the blind singer named Ramanaka from the Rajakingaras and informs him that he has come from the world of the sighted. But he refuses to believe it and is warned that if he spreads such a thing here, he will be killed by the king and the people.
